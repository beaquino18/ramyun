About Us

At Tezukuri Lab, we bring authentic Japanese cuisine directly to your doorstep. "Tezukuri" (手作り) means handmade in Japanese, reflecting our commitment to craft each dish with care and precision, just as it's done in Japan.

Founded by passionate Japanese culinary experts, we specialize in preparing customized bento boxes and authentic ramen using fresh, Japanese-sourced ingredients. Our professional chefs create each order in real-time, ensuring restaurant-quality meals delivered straight to your home.
